Meet Our Faculty

Private lessons for all ages and levels in piano, strings, woodwinds, and voice. Study classical, jazz, or contemporary styles with our experienced faculty.

Piano

Dr. Michael Lehtinen

School Director

Dr. Michael Lehtinen

DMA and MM from the Eastman School of Music; BM from Oberlin Conservatory of Music. Dr. Lehtinen has been teaching at Southern Park Music School since 1995 and has been the Director since 2006. His students participate in festivals and competitions throughout Charlotte and North Carolina. In addition to teaching piano students of all ages, Dr. Lehtinen is the Organist at St. Stephen’s United Methodist Church.

Dr. Amy Harris

Instructor of Piano

Dr. Amy Harris

DMA from University of North Carolina at Greensboro, MM and BMus from University of Georgia. Dr. Harris has taught private piano lessons in Charlotte since 2005. Dr. Harris teaches students of all ages and skill levels, and her goal as a piano instructor is to teach her students every skill they will need to confidently perform, understand, and value the music they play. She is a Nationally Certified Teacher of Music, a Registered Suzuki Piano Teacher, and a member of MTNA and the Charlotte Piano Teachers Forum. Most of all, Dr. Harris enjoys teaching her students and sharing in the joys of their musical growth and endeavors.

Woodwinds

Elaina Palada

Instructor of Flute

Elaina Palada

MM from Northwestern University; BM from Syracuse University. Elaina moved to the Charlotte area in September 2022 to work for Flute World, a flute specialty shop in Charlotte. In addition to working full-time, Elaina enjoys an active career as a performer and teacher. She has performed with several orchestras, and is a substitute flutist for multiple regional orchestras. She is also an in-demand performer for various churches throughout the Carolinas. Originally from Central New York, Elaina has been teaching flute lessons for more than five years and loves sharing her passion for flute with students of all ages!
